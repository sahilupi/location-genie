Hello and welcome to the Location Genie Community! This is a place where hosts and renters can come together to share their ideas, delights, as well as frustrations from their experiences using the platform.

Here are some examples of ways you can use the Location Genie Community:

  • Post questions to Location Genie as well as other hosts on the platform;
  • Share your feedback with the Location Genie Product Team about your experience using the website;
  • Answer other hosts’ questions and share your hosting tips and tricks;
  • Network with other hosts in the community.

We hope that sharing and reading others’ insights will help you achieve more successful bookings as well as help us improve the product as a whole.
