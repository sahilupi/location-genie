What is the cancellation and refund policy for renters and hosts?

We developed the Location Genie cancellation policy to provide flexibility for renters while also providing security for our hosts.
​

Once a property booking is confirmed by the host, the following cancellation and refund rules apply to renters:
​
​
1. All confirmed bookings adhere to Location Genie's Grace Period policy, which allows a full refund for bookings cancelled within 24 hours of confirmation with the exception of bookings that were confirmed within 48 hours of the start time.
​
​
2. If you cancel outside of 48 hours from the start of the booking, you will receive a 50% refund.
​
​
3. Cancellations within 48 hours of the start time are non-refundable.
​
​
The host is entitled to a portion of their payout depending on when the renter cancels the booking.
​
​
In all cancellations, Location Genie retains its standard fees.

If a booking is cancelled outside of the 24-hour grace period, but more than 48 hours before the start of the booking, 50% of the add-ons fee is refunded. If a booking is cancelled less than 48 hours before the start of the booking, the add-ons fee is non refundable.

If you wish to cancel, reach out to us at contact@locationenie.com with your name and the date of your booking and we will effectuate the cancellation per our cancellation guidelines.
​
Refunds to renters will only apply when a cancellation has been made. Please pay careful attention to the cancellation policy before completing a booking request so you are aware of the refunds eligible in the event of a cancellation.
​
It can take up to 6-10 business days for a refund to reach the renter's bank account.

For full details on our cancellation policy, including details related to host-initiated cancellations, please familiarize yourself with our latest Terms of Service.
