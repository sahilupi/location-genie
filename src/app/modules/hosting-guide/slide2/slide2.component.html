How do renters find your location?

Only Location Genie offers full-text search, enabling tenants to look for rentals by style, name, location, or other criteria.

Make sure to highlight anything special or noteworthy about your item in the description or title to increase your chances of getting noticed in searches.
Ensure that you choose every element that your house possesses. Avoid choosing features or fashions that are unrelated to your listing. This will make it more difficult to locate you.
