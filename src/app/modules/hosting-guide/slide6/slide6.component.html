Location Genie Inbox

Renters interested in your property will chat with you or contact you through your inbox. Never forget to reply to our emails, texts, or Location Genie messages. Your reaction time and response rating will improve if you respond fast to every chat, which will help you rank better in search. We take your lack of response seriously and may suspend your account.

You should always ask the following from the renter if they do not provide the information:

  • What do you plan to use the space for?
  • Which areas of the property are you interested in using?
  • Do you have insurance?
  • Do you plan on getting a permit?
  • Are you comfortable with the listed rate?
