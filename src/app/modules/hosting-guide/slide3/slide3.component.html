Good and bad host behavior

Our guiding principle is to reward our top hosts by making it simpler to find and book their listings. As a result, we developed a "listing score." Your location's likelihood of ranking first on Location Genie search pages increases with score, which directly affects the number of inquiries and bookings you receive.

Things that will improve your score:

  • Bookings & Reviews
  • Responding to every inquiry
  • Keeping all communication
  • Being a Superhost

Things that will hurt your score (and may result in suspension):

  • Booking offline
  • Being unresponsive
